Alpha Phi Omega - AEZ

Alpha Phi Omega - AEZ is a coed, all-inclusive community service fraternity at The University of Texas at Dallas dedicated to the three cardinal principles of leadership, friendship, and service.

Be a Leader


Leadership is the first tenet of Alpha Phi Omega. With leadership opportunities like officer positions (including exclusive positions for new members) and exclusive workshops throught the APO LEADS program, we provide you with the opportunity to shape yourself into the leader you want to be

Be a Friend


Above all, Alpha Phi Omega - AEZ values friendship and brotherhood. To foster brotherhood and good fellowship, Alpha Phi Omega - AEZ holds events centered around becoming better friends with existing members. These events may include attending arcades, movie premieres, videogame competitions, cooking tournaments, and--the highlight of every semester--retreat.

Be of Service


As a service organization, Alpha Phi Omega - AEZ excels in its commitment to community service. With exclusive service events including chapter-organized service events, we can help you get your hours, connect you to your community, and even just kill time on the weekend!
