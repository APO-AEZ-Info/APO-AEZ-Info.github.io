HOW TO JOIN >>

Be a Leader, Be a Friend, Be of Service

Since its founding in 1925, Alpha Phi Omega has grown to be the largest community service fraternity in the United States

Leadership

APO-AEZ hosts leadership a diverse range of leadership opportunities. From leadership positions for both active and new members to chapter-hosted leadership workshops--including those through APO's APO LEADS program--we provide the opportunity to grow and challenge yourself as a leader (as well a score a few points for your resume)

Friendship

APO-AEZ is a great way to make friends. Through weekly chapter organized fellowship events including arcades, nature walks, and more; large group-based service events, and our semesterly retreat, you will have the opportunity to connect and bond with all of our APO brothers

Service

The hardest part of getting involved in your community is finding an entry point and friends to embark with you on your mission. Here at APO, we provide many ways to get involved! We host semesterly "Big Service" events to give back to the DFW are, scrape the web for new volunteer opportunities, and get exclusive access to some of the coolest service events in the area.
